Hydroponics 101

What is Hydroponics?

Farming without soil. It sounds like science fiction, but it is actually the most efficient way to grow plants today.

Soil is just a holder.
Water carries the nutrients.

In traditional farming, plants must grow massive root systems to search the soil for water and nutrients. This wastes energy.

With hydroponics, roots are suspended in a perfectly balanced, oxygenated water-nutrient solution. Since everything they need is delivered directly to them, plants grow faster, healthier, and larger—all in a fraction of the space.

💡
Did you know? Hydroponics uses up to 95% less water because the water is continuously recycled within a closed loop system!

Simple Mechanics

How Farmspherica Works

Three easy steps to fresh, clean greens right inside your living room.

01

Nourish (Water & Nutrients)

Add water and organic nutrients to the bottom sphere reservoir. A micro-pump runs automatically, coating the roots with oxygenated nutrient mist.

02

Photosynthesize (Smart LED)

Farmspherica’s built-in, low-energy LED spectrum mimics natural sunlight. It activates automatically based on what plant species you are growing.

03

Harvest & Enjoy

Watch your greens mature in record time. Snip fresh lettuce, spinach, basil, or strawberries directly from the dome whenever you are ready to eat!
